About

STARGLOW debuted on January 21, 2026, formed through BMSG's THE LAST PIECE audition. The 5-member group made their official major debut with 'Star Wish,' which reached Oricon #1. Their 2nd single 'USOTSUKI' (Apr 2026) reached Oricon #3, establishing them as the latest BMSG success story.
