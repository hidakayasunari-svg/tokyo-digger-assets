About

Novel Core (real name: Satake Taro, born Jan 18, 2001, Tokyo) occupies a singular position in Japan's hip-hop landscape. He won the BAZOOKA!!! High School RAP Championship in 2017 and signed with BMSG in 2020. His music confronts themes of identity, loss, alienation, and resilience with lyrical sophistication far beyond typical J-Pop. His debut arena concert at K-Arena Yokohama (Feb 11, 2025, capacity 20,000) marked a major milestone in his career trajectory.
