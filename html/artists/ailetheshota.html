About

Aile The Shota (real name: Watanabe Shota, born Mar 15, 1998, Tokyo) brings a rare R&B sensibility shaped by global influences. He participated in THE FIRST audition before signing with BMSG as a solo artist. His artist name was announced at a SKY-HI concert in September 2021. His vocal style — warm, conversational, technically sophisticated — draws from city pop and soul traditions while remaining distinctly modern. Albums 'REAL POP' (Nov 2024) and 'REAL POP2' (Feb 2026) establish him as a consistent album artist. He hosts 'Aile The Shota no LOVERADIO' on 文化放送 since March 2026.
