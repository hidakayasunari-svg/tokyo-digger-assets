About

Formed through THE FIRST reality audition on ABEMA TV, BE:FIRST debuted in November 2021 as BMSG's flagship group. All members actively contribute to songwriting, composition, and choreography — a rare distinction in J-Pop. In 2025, they completed their first world tour across 12 cities in the US, Europe, and Asia. In May 2026, they headline Ajinomoto Stadium for 110K+ ticket holders — one of Japan's largest outdoor venues.

Members
SOTA
Main Dancer · Vocalist · Center
Born Jan 18, 2001, Fukuoka. Dance ace who started training under his mother, a dance instructor. J-Pop's most technically accomplished dancer with bicultural stage command.
SHUNTO
Main Vocalist · Leader
Born Sep 1, 2003. Emotional anchor of BE:FIRST. Dynamic vocal range spanning ballads to high-energy dance-pop. Calm, deliberate leader who sets the group's tone.
MANATO
Sub-Vocalist · Dancer
Born Apr 29, 2001, Tokyo. Former competitive dancer with theatrical precision and warm vocal tone. Has modeled professionally. BE:FIRST's quiet professional.
RYUHEI
Lead Dancer · Rapper
Born Nov 7, 2006, Nagoya. Youngest member. Rooted in dance and hip-hop. Freestyle background gives live performances improvisational energy.
JUNON
Main Vocalist · Visual
Born May 23, 1998, Tokyo. Oldest member. Fashion ambassador: Louis Vuitton, Prada, Moncler, Gucci, Valentino. HOUSE OF SOLO (UK) cover — global style icon.
LEO
Main Rapper · Lyricist
Born 1998. Active songwriter and composer for BE:FIRST. Introspective lyricism with kinetic delivery. Rare songwriting involvement in J-Pop.
