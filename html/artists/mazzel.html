About

Formed through the MISSION×2 audition program, MAZZEL debuted on May 17, 2023 as BMSG's second boy group. Led by national breakdancing champion SEITO, the 8-member group has established Japan's strongest reputation for live performance intensity. Their fan community MUZEUM is among the most dedicated in BMSG's ecosystem. Their 2nd album 'Banquet' (Apr 2026) demonstrates sustained creative momentum.
