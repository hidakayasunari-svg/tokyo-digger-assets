About

HANA debuted on April 2, 2025 as the first group from BMSG's B-RAVE division, produced by CHANMINA. Formed through the No No Girls global audition (7,000+ applicants), the 7-member group won the 67th Japan Record Award for Best New Artist just 8 months after debuting — one of the fastest ascents in recent J-Pop history.
